The Reasons Why I Put Those Images Of Underground Rappers

We believe ideas come from everyone, everywhere. In fact, at Underground FAM, everyone within our company walls is either a designer or artist in their own right. And there are a few principles we believe—and we believe everyone should believe in the joining of these two crafts. These two ideas drive us, motivate us, and ultimately help us redefine the power of art and design along with music. We’re big believers in doing right by our expressive people. After all, we grew up in New York City ad we believe this place has so much to offer. So we do what we can to support the community we love very much. Over time, we’re hoping to provide more connections between the music and fashion community for up and coming designers and artists, as a way to push them even further into this industry, already having their name in the midst.
